THE BOSTON NAPPING SYMPOSIUM

September 23rd thru the 25th at Boston Convention and Exhibition Center ( Map )

This 3 day event focuses on the techniques that can be implimented and used during short or long term naps.

Guest speakers include the well know DeSouza family:
Henry DeSouza, a male 2yr old pug. Henry is currently in training and completing his residency under the direction of Dr. Albert DeSouza.
Sophie DeSouza, a 16 month old female pug. Sophie is a natural at napping, she currently holds a position of Senior Napping Assistant.
Dr. Albert DeSouza, a 14yr old male pug. Albert holds his Phd. in Napping and is known world-wide for his napping prowess. It has been said that he has napped over more than half his life.
